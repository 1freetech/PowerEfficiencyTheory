# Pricing Ideas

## Goal
Turn Power Efficiency Theory from an interesting concept into a paid asset.

## Offer ladder

### 1. Entry product — Quick Mining Efficiency Snapshot
**Price range:** $19 to $49

What the buyer gets:
- a short scenario summary
- one chart or output table
- 3 to 5 takeaway bullets
- one recommendation section

Why this works:
- low-friction
- easy to test demand
- good for converting curious readers into buyers

### 2. Mid-tier product — Premium Theory Brief
**Price range:** $79 to $199

What the buyer gets:
- a polished research brief
- scenario interpretation
- key assumptions
- applied implications for mining hardware or Bitcoin network analysis

Why this works:
- fits a premium niche product
- useful for Bitcoin-native readers and small operators
- can double as sponsor bait and lead magnet

### 3. Higher-ticket product — Custom Mining Model Review
**Price range:** $250 to $1,500+

What the buyer gets:
- a custom interpretation of client assumptions
- scenario comparisons
- memo or strategy note
- optional follow-up discussion

Why this works:
- monetizes the theory as advisory work
- positions the repo as a proof-of-thinking asset
- can become repeat business

### 4. Sponsor-backed asset
**Price range:** negotiated

What the buyer gets:
- a sponsor-aligned explainer
- branded research framing
- educational credibility tied to mining efficiency

Why this works:
- converts the theory into a media/commercial product
- strongest if paired with BitcoinVersus publishing

## Fastest monetization path
Best order of operations:
1. create one polished premium brief
2. create one small paid snapshot offer
3. create one sponsor-facing pitch angle
4. use outputs to test interest before building a larger dashboard product
