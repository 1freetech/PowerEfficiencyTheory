# Sponsor Pitch Angles

## Why sponsors might care
Power Efficiency Theory can give Bitcoin mining companies and infrastructure brands a more serious, differentiated way to talk about efficiency, productivity, and long-term hardware value.

## Angle 1 — Educational authority
Pitch:
- sponsor content that teaches readers how to think about mining hardware beyond surface marketing numbers

Why it works:
- brands want authority
- this framework sounds more analytical and less promotional

## Angle 2 — Efficiency storytelling
Pitch:
- explain how efficiency improvements translate into stronger computational energy productivity over time

Why it works:
- this connects product specs to economic meaning
- useful for mining hardware, hosting, and infrastructure companies

## Angle 3 — Investor interpretation
Pitch:
- use the framework to explain why hardware efficiency and computational productivity matter to investor-style analysis

Why it works:
- elevates the content above generic product promotion
- attractive for premium readers and serious operators

## Angle 4 — Data-backed premium report
Pitch:
- a sponsor-supported report using scenario outputs and theory-based framing

Why it works:
- strong alignment between technical content and monetization
- gives sponsors a credible, reusable content asset

## Practical sponsor categories
- ASIC manufacturers
- hosting providers
- mining pools
- firmware or optimization providers
- mining analytics platforms
- Bitcoin infrastructure brands

## Simple outreach positioning
Possible positioning sentence:
> Power Efficiency Theory offers a differentiated framework for understanding Bitcoin mining productivity through the combined lens of hashrate growth, efficiency improvement, and energy economics.

- one premium brief
